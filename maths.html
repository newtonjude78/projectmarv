Algebra is the study of mathematical relationships and structures using variables (like $x$ and $y$).

Solving Linear Equations

To solve for $x$ in $3x + 5 = 14$:

  1. Subtract 5 from both sides: $3x = 14 - 5 \Rightarrow 3x = 9$
  2. Divide by 3: $x = \frac{9}{3} \Rightarrow x = 3$

Key Example: Factorisation

Factorise the expression $x^2 + 5x + 6$.

Solution: $(x + 2)(x + 3)$
